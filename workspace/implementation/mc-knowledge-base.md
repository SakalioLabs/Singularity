# Minecraft Crafting Knowledge Base — Early Game Recipes

## Crafting Table: 4 planks -> 1 crafting_table
## Sticks: 2 planks (vertical) -> 4 sticks
## Wooden Pickaxe: 3 planks + 2 sticks
## Wooden Axe: 3 planks + 2 sticks (L-shape)
## Wooden Sword: 2 planks + 1 stick
## Wooden Shovel: 1 plank + 2 sticks
## Stone Pickaxe: 3 cobblestone + 2 sticks
## Stone Axe: 3 cobblestone + 2 sticks
## Stone Sword: 2 cobblestone + 1 stick
## Furnace: 8 cobblestone
## Torch: 1 coal/charcoal + 1 stick
## Bed: 3 wool + 3 planks
## Chest: 8 planks
## Iron Pickaxe: 3 iron_ingot + 2 sticks
## Iron Sword: 2 iron_ingot + 1 stick
## Shield: 6 planks + 1 iron_ingot

## Key Tool Progression
hand -> wooden_pickaxe -> stone_pickaxe -> iron_pickaxe -> diamond_pickaxe
hand -> wooden_axe -> stone_axe -> iron_axe

## Y-Level Guide (1.20.4)
- Iron: most common at y=16, range -64 to 72
- Coal: most common at y=96, range 0 to 320
- Diamond: most common at y=-59, range -64 to 16
- Redstone: y=-63 to 15
- Gold: y=-64 to 32
- Lapis: y=-64 to 64
- Copper: y=-16 to 112

## Hostile Mob Behavior
- Zombie: Burns in sunlight, melee, slow
- Skeleton: Burns in sunlight, ranged arrows
- Spider: Neutral in daylight, climbs walls
- Creeper: Explodes on proximity, no burn in sun
- Enderman: Neutral unless looked at, teleports
- Witch: Throws potions, resistant to poison
